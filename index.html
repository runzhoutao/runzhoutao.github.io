Runzhou Tao (陶润洲)

Runzhou Tao is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Maryland, College Park and a Fellow at the Joint Center for Quantum Information and Computer Science (QuICS).

Runzhou obtained his Ph.D. in 2024 from the Department of Computer Science at Columbia Unversity. He was advised by Prof. Ronghui Gu. He completes his Bachelor's Degree from Yao Class of Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University.

His research interest is building software systems (programming languages, operating systems and formal verification) for quantum computers.

I am looking for motivated PhD students / RAs / Postdocs. If you are interested in Quantum Computing, Formal Verification, Programming Languages or Operating Systems, please feel free to send me an email!
