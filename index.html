Runzhou Tao (陶润洲)

Runzhou Tao is an Assistant Professor of Computer Science at University of Maryland and a Fellow at QuICS. He obtained his Ph.D. from Columbia University in 2024, advised by Ronghui Gu, and his Bachelor's from Yao Class, Tsinghua University.

He builds software stack (PL, OS, verification) for quantum computers.

I am looking for motivated PhD students / RAs / Postdocs. If you are interested in Quantum Computing, Formal Verification, Programming Languages or Operating Systems, please feel free to send me an email!
